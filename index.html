Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services and process.

Do you work with all genres?
Yes. I have successfully launched books in every major genre: romance, thriller, mystery, fantasy, science fiction, young adult, literary fiction, women's fiction, business, self-help, memoir, health, parenting, and more. Different genres require different strategies, which is why every campaign is customized.
Do you work with self-published authors?
Absolutely! The majority of my clients are self-published indie authors. I also work with traditionally published authors who want to supplement their publisher's marketing efforts.
What if my book is already published?
Many of my most successful campaigns have been for books that were already published but not performing well. It is never too late to implement effective marketing. I can help revive and reposition books published months or even years ago.
How long does it take to see results?
Timeline depends on what results you are measuring. Reviews typically start appearing within 1-2 weeks. Social media growth begins within 2-4 weeks. Amazon ranking improvements can be seen within days of launch. Sales momentum typically builds over 2-8 weeks.
Can you guarantee my book will be a bestseller?
No one can ethically guarantee specific results. What I do guarantee is proven strategic marketing tactics, professional execution, transparent communication, and my dedicated effort toward your success. My track record speaks for itself: 500+ successful launches and 50+ bestseller rankings achieved.
Why do you only communicate via email?
Email communication benefits you through complete documentation, time zone flexibility, thoughtful detailed responses, accessibility of records, and professional boundaries. Everything is in writing, searchable, and permanent.
How quickly do you respond to emails?
For initial inquiries, I respond within 24-48 business hours. During active campaigns, I respond within 4-24 hours depending on urgency. Weekends and holidays may have slightly delayed response times, but I always prioritize clear, timely communication.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. For larger packages, I offer payment plans with terms discussed during the proposal phase. This allows you to spread the investment over the campaign timeline. Email me with your budget and I will suggest the best approach for your situation.
