About Patricia

The Strategist Behind Your Author Brand

I'm Patricia Laura — a book marketing strategist who partners exclusively with indie and small-press authors. My work sits at the intersection of publishing intelligence, reader psychology, and platform strategy.

Most authors have extraordinary stories trapped behind weak discoverability. My job is to change that — with tailored campaigns, review strategies, and platform growth plans that create lasting momentum.

Personalised marketing blueprints for your genre & audience
Goodreads, Amazon & social platform optimisation
Review generation & ARC reader campaign management
Cold outreach & book blog placement strategy
Author brand identity & messaging development
What I Offer

Services Built for Indie Authors

Every service is crafted around your book, your genre, and your goals — no cookie-cutter packages.

📖
Discoverability Audit

⭐
Review Campaign

Strategic ARC distribution, Goodreads giveaways, and book blogger outreach to build your review count and social proof before and after launch.

📣
Launch Strategy

A 90-day pre-launch and post-launch marketing roadmap covering email, social, ads, and press — timed for maximum impact on release day.

Platform Growth

✍️
Author Branding

Outreach & Placement

Pitching your book to book clubs, libraries, media outlets, and influencer readers who can amplify your reach beyond your existing audience.
