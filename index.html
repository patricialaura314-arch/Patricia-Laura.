About Me

I Am Patricia Laura, and I Help Authors Sell More Books

With over five years of hands on experience in book marketing, I have dedicated my career to one mission: helping authors get the visibility, engagement, and sales their books truly deserve. I understand the publishing landscape inside and out, and I know how overwhelming it can feel for an author to navigate the marketing side of their work alone.

That is exactly why I am here. I work directly with authors through email to develop customized marketing strategies that are built specifically for their book, their audience, and their goals. I do not believe in generic templates or one size fits all approaches. Every book is unique, and every marketing plan I create reflects that uniqueness.

What sets me apart is my commitment to full transparency and documentation. Every strategy discussion, every campaign update, every result report, and every piece of communication between us happens through email. This means you always have a clear, written record of everything we do together. There are no surprises, no hidden processes, and no unanswered questions.
